Our Company

FIL-SINO Manpower Services Inc. was established in 1993 with the vision of connecting Filipino talent with global opportunities. Over the past three decades, we have built a reputation for excellence in overseas employment services.

Our company is fully licensed by the Philippine Overseas Employment Administration (POEA) with License No. 191-LB-070518-R, ensuring that all our operations comply with government regulations and international standards.

We specialize in placing skilled Filipino workers in various industries including healthcare, construction, information technology, hospitality, and manufacturing across different countries worldwide.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide comprehensive and ethical manpower services that bridge the gap between Filipino talent and international employment opportunities.

We are committed to:

  • Ensuring fair and transparent recruitment processes
  • Providing pre-departure training and orientation
  • Maintaining strong relationships with international employers
  • Supporting our workers throughout their overseas employment
  • Upholding the highest standards of professionalism and integrity

Our Vision

Our vision is to be recognized as the premier manpower services company in the Philippines, setting the standard for excellence in overseas employment.

We envision a future where:

  • Filipino workers are globally recognized for their skills and professionalism
  • International employers trust us as their preferred recruitment partner
  • Our services contribute to the economic growth of the Philippines
  • We continue to innovate and adapt to changing global employment trends
  • Every placement we make creates lasting positive impact for all stakeholders
